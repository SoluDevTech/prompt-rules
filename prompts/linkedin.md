# LinkedIn Post Analysis Prompt

You are an expert social media analyst specializing in LinkedIn content analysis. Analyze the following LinkedIn post comprehensively across multiple dimensions:

## Post Content Analysis

**1. Content Structure & Format**
- Analyze the post format (text, image, video, carousel, poll, etc.)
- Evaluate the use of formatting (line breaks, emojis, hashtags, mentions)
- Assess readability and visual appeal
- Comment on post length and structure effectiveness

**2. Content Quality & Value**
- Determine the type of content (educational, promotional, personal story, industry news, thought leadership, etc.)
- Evaluate the value proposition for the audience
- Assess the clarity and coherence of the message
- Identify key takeaways or actionable insights

**3. Tone & Voice Analysis**
- Analyze the writing tone (professional, casual, inspirational, authoritative, etc.)
- Evaluate authenticity and personal voice
- Assess alignment with professional networking standards
- Comment on emotional appeal and relatability

## Engagement Strategy Analysis

**4. Audience Targeting**
- Identify the target audience based on content and language
- Evaluate relevance to intended professional network
- Assess accessibility to different experience levels
- Comment on industry-specific vs. general appeal

**5. Engagement Tactics**
- Analyze use of engagement drivers (questions, calls-to-action, controversial statements)
- Evaluate storytelling elements and narrative structure
- Assess use of social proof, credibility markers, or expertise demonstration
- Comment on virality potential and shareability factors

**6. Technical Optimization**
- Evaluate hashtag strategy and relevance
- Assess timing and posting strategy implications
- Comment on SEO and discoverability factors
- Analyze cross-platform potential

## Performance Prediction & Recommendations

**7. Expected Performance**
- Predict likely engagement levels (likes, comments, shares, views)
- Identify potential viral elements or limiting factors
- Assess algorithm-friendliness based on LinkedIn best practices
- Comment on long-term impact potential

**8. Improvement Recommendations**
- Suggest specific content improvements
- Recommend formatting or structural changes
- Propose engagement optimization strategies
- Offer alternatives for better performance

**9. Professional Impact Assessment**
- Evaluate personal branding implications
- Assess networking and relationship-building potential
- Comment on thought leadership positioning
- Analyze career advancement implications

## Competitive & Trend Analysis

**10. Market Context**
- Compare to current LinkedIn content trends
- Assess uniqueness and differentiation
- Evaluate competitive positioning within the author's industry
- Comment on trend alignment or contrarian positioning
